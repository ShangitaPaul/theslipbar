On the Waterfront in Redondo Beach or in the cozy neighborhood nook of Lomita! We are a friendly local spot specializing in craft and locally brewed beers, spirits, and delicious eats! At The Slip, we don't think the food at a bar has to taste like bar food. In addition to delicious wings, pizza, burgers, and fries, we offer a Blue Zones certified menu with plenty of healthy and vegetarian choices.
